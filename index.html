About the venture

Financially sustainable. Socially impactful.

ReWeave is a social enterprise that addresses women’s economic inclusion and environmental waste at the same time. It creates handmade lifestyle products while building dignified, flexible work opportunities.

ReWeave

Purpose of the logo

The woven basket represents creativity, craftsmanship, and the transformation of discarded materials into useful products. The hands symbolize women artisans, care, and skill. Together, the logo shows ReWeave’s purpose: turning waste into opportunity through flexible, dignified work and sustainable design.

How it works
1

Discover

Customers explore ReWeave through Instagram, online platforms, university fairs, and pop-up stalls.

2

Order

Buyers place orders for handmade décor, accessories, and premium upcycled pieces.

3

Work Flexibly

Women artisans can work from home according to their own flexibility, making it easier to earn while managing family responsibilities.

4

Deliver

Products are packed and delivered while creating both environmental and social impact.

Impact

Creating value for women, communities, and the environment.

Social Impact

ReWeave supports women who face rejection after being out of the workforce for too long, giving them flexible, confidence-building opportunities.

Environmental Impact

The venture reduces landfill waste by upcycling discarded textile and plastic materials into valuable products.

Economic Impact

A sustainable revenue model supports growth through product sales, fairs, pop-ups, and future partnerships.
